# Why Grade-A NPK Matters

When you're looking for the best organic fertiliser for your garden, you'll often see the letters **NPK** followed by some numbers. But what do they actually mean, and why is "Grade-A" quality essential?

## Understanding NPK

NPK stands for the three primary nutrients plants need to thrive:
- **N (Nitrogen):** For lush, green leaf growth.
- **P (Phosphorus):** For strong root development and flower production.
- **K (Potassium):** For overall plant health and disease resistance.
