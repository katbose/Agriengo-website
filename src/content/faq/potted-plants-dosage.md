---
question: "How much vermicompost should I use for potted plants?"
order: 1
category: "Usage"
---

For a standard 12-inch pot, we recommend adding **200-300g** (about two handfuls) of AGRIENGO™ Premium Vermi Compost. Mix it into the top 2-3 inches of soil and water immediately. Repeat every 30-45 days for best results.
